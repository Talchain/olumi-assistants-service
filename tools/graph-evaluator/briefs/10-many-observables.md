We run a subscription meal-kit service with 8,000 active subscribers, 12% monthly churn, average order value of £35, and a Net Promoter Score of 42. Our food waste rate is 8% of ingredients shipped, customer support handles 400 tickets per week, and our ingredient sourcing cost is £14 per box. We're considering switching from weekly to flexible scheduling where customers choose their delivery days. The alternative is keeping weekly deliveries but adding a premium tier with organic-only ingredients at £49 per box.
