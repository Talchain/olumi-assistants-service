Our SaaS platform has three pricing tiers: Basic (£19/mo, 60% of users), Pro (£49/mo, 30%), Enterprise (£149/mo, 10%). We're considering three changes: Option A raises all tiers by 15% (to £22, £56, £171). Option B raises only Enterprise by 30% to £194 while keeping Basic and Pro unchanged. Option C raises Basic and Pro by 10% (to £21, £54) while dropping Enterprise by 5% to £142. We have 5,000 total customers and need to increase MRR from £215k to £250k within 6 months without pushing churn above 5%.
