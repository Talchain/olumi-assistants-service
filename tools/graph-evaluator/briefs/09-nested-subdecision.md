We're a 200-person logistics company deciding whether to build our own last-mile delivery fleet or partner with third-party couriers. If we build, we need to choose between electric vans (£45k each, 20 needed) or diesel vans (£30k each, 20 needed). If we partner, we're choosing between exclusive contracts with one courier at £8 per delivery or non-exclusive contracts with three couriers at £10 per delivery. Current delivery volume is 3,000 per day at £12 average revenue per delivery. We need to reduce our cost-per-delivery below £7 within 12 months while maintaining our 98% on-time rate. The transport regulator is expected to announce new emissions standards in Q3 that could affect diesel fleet viability.
